# Interview Rubric

Ask one question at a time. Provide a recommended answer when useful.

Clarify:

- problem: what pain, gap, or opportunity exists
- user: who has the problem
- current workaround: what they do today
- proposed solution: what changes
- unique angle: why this is different
- scope: what is included and excluded
- success: what would prove it worked
- constraints: time, data, budget, hardware, skills, policy
- risks: what could make it fail
- assumptions: what must be true

Stop when you can restate the idea as:

```text
For <user>, who struggles with <problem>, build <solution>, because <why now>. Success means <signal>. The main assumptions are <assumptions>.
```
